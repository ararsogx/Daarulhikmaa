About Us

Our Mission

To nurture Muslim youth through holistic education, financial empowerment, and spiritual guidance, rooted in Islamic values.

Our Vision

A generation of confident, compassionate, and contributing Muslims who embody the teachings of Islam in their daily lives.

Our Journey

2010

Foundation

Darul Hikma was established by a group of educators and community leaders.

2013

First Youth Camp

Launched our annual summer camp, now serving over 200 youth each year.

2017

Scholarship Program

Started providing financial aid for higher education; 50+ students supported.

2022

Community Center

Opened a dedicated center offering classes, mentorship, and events.
