About Us

Our Mission

To nurture the youth of Fadis Woreda, East Hararge, through quality education rooted in Islamic values, empowering them to become confident and contributing members of society.

Our Vision

A generation of educated, compassionate, and faithful Muslims from Fadis who lead their communities with wisdom and integrity.

Our Journey in Fadis

2010

Foundation

Darul Hikma was established by local educators and community leaders in Fadis.

2013

First Tutoring Program

Launched after‑school tutoring for primary school students.

2017

Scholarship Program

Started providing financial aid for higher education to Fadis students; 50+ supported.

2022

Community Center

Opened a dedicated center in Fadis offering classes, mentorship, and events.
