Education Program

Empowering Minds, Building Futures

Our Education program is designed to support Muslim youth at every stage of their academic journey. We believe that education is the key to breaking cycles of poverty and building strong, confident communities.

Through scholarships, after-school tutoring, and STEM workshops, we help students excel academically while staying connected to their Islamic identity. Our dedicated volunteers and educators provide personalized attention to ensure no child is left behind.

Key Features

  • Need-based scholarships for K-12 and college
  • One-on-one tutoring in core subjects
  • STEM camps and robotics clubs
  • College application mentorship
  • Islamic studies integration
