Charity Program

Sharing Blessings, Spreading Hope

Our Charity program is a channel for your Zakat and Sadaqah to reach those who need it most. We organize food drives, winter clothing distributions, and support for local masjids, ensuring that your donations make a tangible difference in our community.

We follow strict Shariah compliance and transparency, providing donors with detailed reports on how their funds are used.

Key Initiatives

  • Monthly food boxes for low-income families
  • Winter coat and blanket drives
  • Zakat distribution to eligible individuals
  • Support for local masjid programs
  • Emergency disaster relief
