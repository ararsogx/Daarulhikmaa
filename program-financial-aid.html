Financial Aid Program

Support When It Matters Most

Our Financial Aid program provides a safety net for Muslim families facing economic hardship. Rooted in the Islamic principles of qard hasan (benevolent loan) and sadaqah, we offer interest-free loans and emergency grants to help families overcome temporary challenges.

We also conduct financial literacy workshops to empower individuals with budgeting, saving, and debt management skills – all from an Islamic perspective.

Key Features

  • Interest-free loans (qard hasan) up to $2,000
  • Emergency grants for urgent needs (rent, utilities, medical)
  • Financial literacy workshops
  • Zakat distribution assistance
  • Confidential and compassionate counseling
